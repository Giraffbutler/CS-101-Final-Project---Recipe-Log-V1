Description:
Fresh, homemade bread that reminds you of home.  Anything can be added to the base outlined below to add flavor or style. 

Good combos include:
Jalepeno Cheddar
Cardamom, Cinnamon, Honey, and Cranberry

***
Ingredients List:

3 1/4 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp yeast
1 1/2 cup water

***
Directions:

1. Mix the flour, salt, and yeast and other dry components (if doing a variation) together in a large bowl
2. Heat water to 108 Fahrenheit in saucepan
3. Add heated water to dry mixture
4. Mix in water until dough forms. Add more heated water as needed
5. Once dough formed, keep in mixing bowl and cover with cloth to let rise for >8 hours
6. If letting rise for long period of time (>12 hrs) beat down dough at 8 hr mark and let rise again
7. Once risen, remove dough from mixing bowl and fold into ball on parchment paper
8. Set oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Pray dutch oven for 30 minutes at temperature prior to baking
9. Add dough (still on parchment paper) to the prayed dutch oven and bake for 30 minutes at 450
10. Remove lid and bake for another 4-6 minutes at 450
11. Remove from the oven and let rest for 4-5 minutes.  Enjoy! The secret works! updoot
